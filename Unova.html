Unova Region

Information on Unova Region

The Unova Region is featured in the game Pokémon Black and White. It was also featured in the games sequel Pokémon Black and White 2.
The Unova Region shares similarity to New York City. The starter pokémon that came with this game is Tepig, Snivy and, Oshawott.
They are your typical starter pokémon that are fire, water and grass type and have 2 other evolution for the player to evolve
