Kalos Region

Information on Kalos Region

Kalos Region is a Pokémon region that is featured in Pokémon X and Y. This region shares similarity with France.
The starter of this Pokémon X and Y is Chespin, Fennekin, and Froakie. They are your usual starter Pokémon with grass,
water and fire type. As well they can evolved twice to make your Pokémon stronger. Some notable location that can be
found in the region is Luminose City, which is the big city in the middle of the map, and Snowbelle city that can be found snow biome.
